Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

What we collect

We collect your email address when you sign up, which is used only for authentication and delivering your daily news digest. We store your feed preferences (which categories you follow, display settings) so they persist across sessions.

How we use it

Your email is used to send you a one-time sign-in code and, if enabled, a daily news digest. We do not sell, share, or use your email for advertising. Your preferences are used solely to personalise your feed.

Data storage

Account data is stored securely via Supabase. News content is fetched from public RSS feeds and processed daily — we do not store your reading history or track which stories you read.

Third-party services

We use Supabase for authentication and database storage, and Resend for transactional email delivery. These services have their own privacy policies governing their handling of data.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account at any time from the Account panel in the app. This permanently removes your email address and all stored preferences from our systems.
